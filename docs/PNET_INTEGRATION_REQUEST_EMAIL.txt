- **Application Endpoint**: Submitting complete candidate applications
- **Question Types**: All supported field types (INPUT_TEXT, INPUT_INT, TEXTAREA, DATE, DATE_RANGE, SINGLE_SELECT, MULTI_SELECT, RADIO, CHECKBOX, FILE, HYPERLINK, INFORMATION)

- **Consent Management**: Mandatory and optional consents
- **CV Submission**: Base64-encoded documents (PDF, DOCX, DOC, RTF, ODT, TXT)
- **AI Processing**: Automatic generation of intelligent, context-aware answers to screening questions
